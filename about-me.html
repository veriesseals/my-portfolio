ABOUT ME

My life began in the opposite way of software development. I always loved art. Traditional Art studies and video games consumed most of my life. Kindergarten through college I would draw, paint, sculpt, study graphic design and more.

The first experience I had with a personal computer was my 11th grade year in high school. I don’t remember the brand of computers that we used but it was my first experience with DOS. It seemed so high tech back then. I remember being so nervous to touch it. We were introduced to programming. The programming language we learned to use was called Basic.

It was an amazing experience for me. Basic was hard for me to absorb but I wanted nothing more than to become good at it. I never lost my curiosity for it over all these years. I didn’t get to learn more about programming until a few years ago.

Looking into going back to school, ad’s popped up in my social media feed about Udemy. I started buying programming courses on sale every time I got a chance. I really want to learn to be a Full Stack Web Developer, so I have to get this down!
