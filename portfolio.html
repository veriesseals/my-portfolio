About My Portfolio

My portfolio is a combination of practice projects and studies created from my Udemy courses and my course work from various programming bootcamps. These projects show the growth of my skills. From my fledgling days when I felt like I had no clue what I am doing but I love it, to this point where I feel like I have a somewhat better grasp on this programming thing.

I still feel I have a lot to learn, but I am proud of the body of work that I have produced. I can see the fruits of my labor. I hope that as you browse my portfolio you can also see my effort and appreciate my work at the same time.
